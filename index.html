Rosa, a biological researcher in a microbiology laboratory, has partnered with Improbable Beef, a food start-up, for human biodiversity. They are trying to find a species of bacteria within the human navel that can synthesizeproteins to create a "next best taste" for beef. Samples were taken from the navels of individuals across the country.

If you were one of those participants, welcome! Please select your participant ID number within the drop-down list. The bar graph will reflect the most populous bacteria found within your navel. The scatter plot will reflectthe various bacteria cultures analyzed. And, just for fun, there is a gauge chart which shows how many times you wash your navel per week.
